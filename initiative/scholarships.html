Scholarships to Escuela Agrícola Panamericana (Zamorano)

In 2006, LAAD started a program to grant scholarships to students attending the Escuela Agrícola Panamericana (Zamorano) in Honduras. Several of LAAD’s executives have graduated from the college and through their work, LAAD saw value in how the school prepared young men and women to understand the technical, managerial and marketing aspects of agricultural production in order to be able to contribute significantly to their communities upon graduation. LAAD currently finances half scholarships to four students every year. The company has already supported 13 students through this mechanism and will continue to do so in the future. The four scholarship recipients for year 2015 have sent LAAD small notes expressing their gratitude for this assistance (see letters below).
